About Me

Nice to meet you, my name is Naomi. Currently, I work as a PR professional at a fashion retail company in Tokyo. I believe that digital communication will continue to play a significant role in the media world, so I have added studying at SAIT in Canada to my life's tasks in order to deepen my knowledge in this field.

My professional career

I have been active in the fashion industry for many years. Initially, I worked as a salesperson at a renowned maison brand in Tokyo, later taking on the role of a buyer as well, which gave me numerous opportunities to immerse myself in European fashion culture. After majoring in fashion marketing at an arts university in London UK, I am currently working in public relations for a fashion retail company in Japan.

What I notice in Canada

What I noticed upon arriving in Canada is that in this diverse country, I encountered an abundance of talented individuals. While attending SAIT, I met many people who already possessed professional skills. I witnessed firsthand that Canada is not fully utilizing this talent.

Next step is...

I wonder how many offers these individuals with such skills would receive in Japan, especially in Tokyo. It feels like a significant waste. Therefore, I am determined to leverage this network of connections I have built in Canada in the future.
